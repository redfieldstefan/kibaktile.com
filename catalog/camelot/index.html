Camelot

Timeless Camelot, originally part of our Euro- Classic line for Ann Sacks, Camelot easily transforms a kitchen or bath from routine to showstopper. This pattern adds color and interest to the walls/floors of any Contemporary, Traditional, Craftsman, or Farmhouse style interior. Choose from our Warm, Cool, Neutral, or white color ways, OR infuse a bit of your own color story to create a truly personal look.

Suitable for interior wall, countertop and low-traffic floors. Exterior use in non-freezing climates only. Tile dimensions are 6" x 6" and 8" x 8".
