Wallflower

Another classic Kibak Tile Sakura pattern, what can we say? We love the all-around utility of these kimono inspired patterns. Go for a pop of happy color in the laundry room or powder room, or infuse a little urbane elegance with our more organic, earthy, specialty glazes.

Suitable for interior wall, countertop and low-traffic floors. Exterior use in non-freezing climates only. Tile dimensions are 6" x 6" and 8" x 8".
